## Academic profile

**Enrique Fernandez-Blanco** is an Associate Professor at the **University of A Coruña**, affiliated with the **Department of Computer Science and Information Technologies**, **CITIC**, and the **RNASA-IMEDIR** research group.

His research focuses on **applied artificial intelligence**, with particular interest in:

- **AI for medicine and health**
- **signal analysis and intelligent sensing**
- **applied AI in real-world domains**
- **sequential decision-making systems**

Methodologically, his work combines **machine learning**, **deep learning**, **evolutionary computation**, and **reinforcement learning**, with emphasis on robust and empirically grounded models.

## Research summary

- **Main areas:** AI for health, signals, applied AI, sequential decision-making
- **Representative topics:** EEG and physiological signals, human activity recognition, harmful algal bloom prediction, symbolic regression, UAV swarm path planning
- **Current emphasis:** practical AI systems for health and complex real-world environments
