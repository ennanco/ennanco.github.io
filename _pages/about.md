**ORCID:** [0000-0003-3260-8734](https://orcid.org/0000-0003-3260-8734)

Enrique Fernandez-Blanco is an **Associate Professor at the University of A Coruña**, affiliated with the **Department of Computer Science and Information Technologies**, **CITIC**, and the **RNASA-IMEDIR** research group.

His work focuses on **applied artificial intelligence** and the design of data-driven methods that are robust, useful, and empirically grounded.

His main research lines are **AI for medicine and health**, **signal analysis and intelligent sensing**, **applied AI across real-world domains**, and **sequential decision-making systems**. Across these areas, he combines machine learning, deep learning, evolutionary computation, and reinforcement learning to build models that support prediction, interpretation, and decision-making in complex settings.

A recurrent goal in his work is to connect methodological development with realistic applications, especially in contexts where model performance, reliability, and practical utility matter.

## Research interests

- Applied Artificial Intelligence
- AI for Medicine and Health
- Signal Analysis and Intelligent Sensing
- Sequential Decision-Making Systems
- Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Reinforcement Learning
